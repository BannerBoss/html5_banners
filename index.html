What i can do...

I am banner whizz, among other things, with over 9 years of experience specializing in stylish animations and interesting interactions, but I'm equally happy to create some quick simple banners as well.

  • * I can create banners to suit your needs:
  • * Built in HTML5 and JavaScript or Flash.
  • * Simple, expanding, rich-media, video, or dynamic text and images.
  • * Stunning code-based animations, integrating with relevant libraries like Greensock.
  • * Metrics to measure the interactions important to you.
  • * Multiple versions, sizes, languages created quickly.
  • * Supplied as standalone or uploaded to DoubleClick, Sizmek, Flashtalking, Google or similar.
  • * I can even create fancy animated backup gifs.

I can work with your in-house designers and bring to life whatever they envisage, or I’m happy to take lead with animation ideas, or I can work with one of my own designers to come up with an idea from scratch.
